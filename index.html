About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Loyola University Chicago, advised by Ifeoma Ozodiegwu, Francisco Iacobelli, and Mohammed Abuhamad. My research focuses on reliable reasoning in AI systems, with specific interests in:

  • Inference-time constraints for tool-using agents
  • Structured reasoning verification
  • Formal methods for AI safety

I am particularly interested in developing methods to ensure AI systems reason correctly when interacting with external tools and real-world data, especially in high-stakes domains like public health.
